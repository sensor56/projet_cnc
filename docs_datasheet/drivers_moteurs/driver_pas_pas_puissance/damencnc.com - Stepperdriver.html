Stepper Drive HP 4015 40V 1,5A driver 1/64 microstep

The 4015 is a very small size high performance microstepping drive. It's suitable for driving any 2-phase and 4-phase hybrid stepping motors. By using advanced bipolar constant-current chopping technique, it can output more speed and power from the same motor, compared with traditional drives such as L/R drives...
